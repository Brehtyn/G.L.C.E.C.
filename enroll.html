All children who enter GLCEC must be at least one year of age.

GLCEC & GLCP are non-discriminatory. We believe in equal attention and care for all children without regards to race, color, religion, national origin, or ancestry.

When your child is enrolled a completed set of emergency forms must be on file. There is an annual registration fee for each child enrolled. A change of clothes is necessary for children of all ages in case of accidents. Guiding Light is not responsible for personal items that are lost or stolen.
