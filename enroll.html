Requirements & Beginning Enrollment






GLCEC & GLCP Inc. are non-discriminatory. We believe in equal attention and care for all children without regards to race, religion, national orgins, or ancestory.

When your child is enrolled a completed set of emergency forms must be on file. A change of clothes is also required by all kids. All programs pay the same flat annual registration fee, but book fees vary by grade.
