Admission

Below is a list of items that are required for registration.

    Requirements & Beginning Enrollment

  1. Enrollment Packet
    Note: All forms MUST be complete with parent/guardian information including: social security number, place of employment, contact numbers, custody document, Emergency Card, ACH form, etc…)
  2. Birth certificate
  3. Shot record
  4. Picture ID (Parents)
  5. $100.00 Registration fee
    (Due at the time of picking up paperwork)

The following items will be needed on the 1st day of school :

    Day 1 Necessities

  1. 1st month/week Tuition
  2. Change of clothes in a Ziploc bag labeled with your child’s name (to keep in cubby)
  3. Diapers, Pull-Ups and Wipes (if necessary)
  4. Water bottle labeled with your child’s name
    (Can ONLY contain water, per state regulation)
  5. Non-Heat up lunch with an ice pack

When your child is enrolled a completed set of emergency forms must be on file. There is an annual registration fee for each child enrolled. A change of clothes is necessary for children of all ages in case of accidents. Guiding Light is not responsible for personal items that are lost or stolen.

Please be sure that all registration forms are completed and returned to Guiding Light at least 24 hours prior to your child’s start date. Without these forms completed your child will not be able to start on your desired date.

Pricing

All programs have a $100.00 annual registration fee.
Our Drop-In-Rate for any varying schedule is $40.00.
Kindergarten and 1st Grade classes have a $140.00 and $160.00 book fee respectively.
All fees are non-refundable.

For non-potty trained children there is an additional $3.00 charge per day.
